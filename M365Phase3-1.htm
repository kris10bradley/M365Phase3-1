What is the M365 File Migration and Apps Modernization project?

Summa Health is continuing the transition to Microsoft 365 (M365) with the goal of simplifying and expediting our organization-wide communications and collaboration through one central, accessible, online platform. The focus of this phase consists of the following:

  Individual files (U: Drives) are moving to OneDrive for Business in M365

  Shared department files (G: Drive) are moving to SharePoint Online in M365

  Microsoft 2016 desktop applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, and Outlook have been upgraded to M365 desktop applications

What is the purpose/benefits of the M365 File Migration and Apps Modernization project?

This initiative will provide employees with a more connected experience by utilizing Microsoft 365.  The M365 desktop application upgrade will ensure all employees have the most up-to-date new features, upgrades, and security updates. The file migration to OneDrive and SharePoint will provide the following benefits all employees: 

  Easier access to individual files and folders

  Simplified sharing and permissions control 

  Automatic saving capabilities

  Enhanced collaboration  

  Co-authoring and co-editing documents even in real time

What is the timeline of the M365 File Migration and Apps Modernization project?

Summa Health has already updated all desktop Office applications from Microsoft 2016 to Microsoft 365.

Most Summa Health employees personal files and folders have been migrated from their U: Drive to OneDrive for Business. The U: Drives of employees with PST files have NOT migrated to OneDrive yet. They will be informed by email of their migration date once it is determined.

The migration of shared department G: Drive file folders to SharePoint in M365 has not begun yet. The date of this migration is to be determined.

Who will be affected by the M365 File Migration and Apps Modernization project?

All Summa Health employees. This transition will not include Summa Care employees.

What happened to my U: Drive files and folders ?

Most Summa Health employees personal files and folders have been migrated from their U: Drive to OneDrive for Business. If you have PST files in your U: Drive, then we have NOT migrated your U: Drive content to OneDrive yet. You will be informed via email of your migration date once it is determined.

What are PST files?

PST files are an email archive storage location accessible through Outlook.

What will happen to my PST files?

You will not lose your PST files. We are currently determining an approved method to transfer all PST files to a new secure location. Once that is done, you will be informed via email. You will also be given a new U: Drive to OneDrive migration date.

For now, please leave your PST files in their current location.

How do I access my U: Drive files after migration to OneDrive?

Employees can access their files in the U: Drive folder in their OneDrive account. They can access their OneDrive through Microsoft 365 online or through File Explorer locally. See this tip sheet for step-by-step instructions.

Im missing some or all of my personal files in OneDrive after my migration.

On rare occasions, after the OneDrive file migration, a folder may get renamed to a slightly different and longer name.

In that instance, do a search for the folder name to find it before assuming it is missing. If the file is, in fact, missing, escalate the issue to Jim Fast or Vincent Odong.

Please note that .pst files will not be migrated to OneDrive.

Why is there an empty U Drive folder in my OneDrive account?

If a user is already using OneDrive, but their U: Drive files have not been migrated yet, they may notice an empty U Drive folder in their account and are told in the communications to leave it there and disregard it. After their migration the folder will contain all of their U:Drive files.
